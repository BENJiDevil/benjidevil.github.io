What is CBD?

What is CBD?

Cannabidiol, also known as CBD, is a 100% natural product derived from the Hemp oil plant. The seeds of the plant contain Omega 3 and Omega 6 fatty acids, as well as vitamins A, B1, B2, B3, B6, C, D and E. Cannabidiol is an incredibly effective ingredient in skin care, contributing to a number of processes in the body and skin.

What is CBD full Spectrum Oi?

Our Full Spectrum Oil includes CBD and all the minor cannabinoids, terpenes and other compounds naturally occurring in the plant. This whole-plant extract allows you to experience the so called ‘entourage’ effect. Through our state of art extraction technology we have removed the THC through Thermal Degrading and thus our Full Spectrum Oils also have 0% THC present.

What to choose

Broad spectrum or Full spectrum is right for you?

There is no right or wrong choice. Choosing the right CBD oil depends on the effect you want to achieve. Each CBD oil has a different effect on your health. CBD Full Spectrum is designed for consumers who don’t bother to take a small dose of THC.

On the other hand, CBD Broad Spectrum is for people who don’t want to consume or are sensitive to THC. It is also suitable for those users, who try CBD oil for the first time. We recommend you to try both CBD oils to decide which one is best for you.
