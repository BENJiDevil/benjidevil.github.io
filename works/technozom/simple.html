Privacy Policy

Return and Exchange Terms

Not satisfied with your product? Or is it broken? That is annoying and we are going to do something about it.

If you are not satisfied with your purchase, you can of course return it. The trial period is 14 days from the day of delivery. You have 14 days from the time of the return notification to return the product. You are responsible for damage or disappearance of the package during the return.

For the return of the order, the return costs are at your expense. We recommend to send the return with track & trace code.

Do you want to return the entire order? Items already received, if unused & undamaged, can be returned to the return address below. We will refund the purchase price minus any shipping costs.

If you return an item or entire order you must always send the fully completed return form (form in Dutch, can be filled in English) and a copy of your invoice.

All cancellations, exchanges and returns, please let us know by email. In this case, please include your full name and order number and the items you wish to cancel, exchange or return. Please note that you can only return your order if it is unused.

Please note: the account number you specify for the refund must be the same as the account number with which you paid your order. This is because of the possible fraud with refunds.

Any amounts already paid will be refunded to you within 14 working days after return notification.

The reimbursement is free of charge for the consumer.

What products can be exchanged or returned

Only items purchased from Technozom.com can be exchanged and returned.

The article must be returned undamaged, unused and in its original packaging. If the items are in a worse condition due to further use, are incomplete, not in the original packaging or are returned with a damaged original packaging, we reserve the right to demand compensation.

Return the item (s) always well packaged (in the original packaging and neatly packed). So do not use the original box as a mail package and stick it to the return address, for example. Just like you, a next customer also likes to receive the item in a neat and original package.

Always keep proof of delivery until you have received confirmation from us that your return has been received. This proof shows the date and the receipt number of your return shipment.

The shipping costs and risks of the return are at the expense of the buyer, unless it concerns a return of an incorrectly delivered item. In the case of an incorrectly delivered item, we will reimburse you for the costs incurred for the return shipment.

If your order is delivered, you have 14 days to exchange or return the items. After this period, the purchase can no longer be dissolved and becomes final. Unstamped or too franked packages are not accepted by Technozom.com, regardless of the reason for the return.
