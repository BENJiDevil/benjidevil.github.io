Privacy Policy

Personal details AVG

This privacy statement applies to the services of Technozom (technozom.com) and indicates how we handle the personal details of our customers.

We are responsible for ensuring that your personal data are processed in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation and in a proper and careful manner.

When you use our services, we ask you to provide personal information. We can also ask you for personal data during the execution of our services. Your personal data are necessary to be able to provide our services to you and for the execution of the agreement concluded with you.

In addition, we use your personal information to inform you about (commercial) offers relating to our services, such as offers of bicycles, (motor) vehicles, accessories, financing or insurances. In connection with this, we can also provide your personal data to third parties. Your personal data can also be provided to third parties if this is necessary for the execution of the agreement, such as the registration of your scooter or invoicing. Your personal data are not used for automatic decision making and are not reinforced outside the EU or international organizations.

If you do not wish to provide us with personal data, you can not use our services.

We keep your personal data on the invoices as long as necessary. (7 years of retention). For the execution of our services, such as carrying out maintenance on your bicycle or (motor) vehicle, or the agreement concluded with you. As soon as the service or the agreement ends, we will retain your personal data for a maximum of 1 month in connection with the settlement of our services or the agreement concluded with you.

You have the right to access your personal data, the right to correct or delete your personal data and you can ask us to limit the processing of your personal data. You can also object to the use of your personal data or you can withdraw your consent to the use of your personal data.

You also have the right to transfer your personal data to, for example, another dealer. This means that we make your personal data available to you in a customary format.

Obviously, we take appropriate technical and organizational measures to prevent the loss of personal data or unlawful processing.

This privacy statement applies to the agreement concluded with you and to the services we provide to you. Changes to our services or laws and regulations may result in changes to this privacy statement. Changes will be made known via our website. We therefore advise you to consult our website regularly at www.technozom.comcompetent court.

What products can be exchanged or returned

Only items purchased from Technozom.com can be exchanged and returned.

The article must be returned undamaged, unused and in its original packaging. If the items are in a worse condition due to further use, are incomplete, not in the original packaging or are returned with a damaged original packaging, we reserve the right to demand compensation.

Return the item (s) always well packaged (in the original packaging and neatly packed). So do not use the original box as a mail package and stick it to the return address, for example. Just like you, a next customer also likes to receive the item in a neat and original package.

Always keep proof of delivery until you have received confirmation from us that your return has been received. This proof shows the date and the receipt number of your return shipment.

The shipping costs and risks of the return are at the expense of the buyer, unless it concerns a return of an incorrectly delivered item. In the case of an incorrectly delivered item, we will reimburse you for the costs incurred for the return shipment.

If your order is delivered, you have 14 days to exchange or return the items. After this period, the purchase can no longer be dissolved and becomes final. Unstamped or too franked packages are not accepted by Technozom.com, regardless of the reason for the return.
