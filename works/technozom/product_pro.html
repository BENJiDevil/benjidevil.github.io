The APP

The Techno PRO comes with an iOS / Android App with which you can view the scooter. Think of your driving pattern, speed, battery charge, updating software, etc.

1

Remote diagnostics

With every meter you ride, your e-scooter receives support, through the air and around the corner. Chat live with our team, anytime.

2

Speed & mileage

This overview gives you insight into your use of the Techno PRO e-scooter, your current speed and the number of kilometers that you are expected to still be able to ride. The same as on the main screen.

3

Cruise Control System

It is a cruise control system that automatically allows the e-scooter to maintain a speed set by the accelerator pedal switch without the need for permanent activation. Available for all options.

4

The lock

The Techno app connects you and your ride. It unlocks the escooter automatically and gives you real-time guidance and support.
